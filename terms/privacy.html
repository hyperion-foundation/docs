What data we collects and what it is used for

  • When you visit *.hyperfoundation.xyz sites (except dashboard.hyperfoundation.xyz)
    • We collect your device information

    We collect some of your device information by using third-party app such as Google Analytics (User Analytic Tool) & Apache HTTP-Server(Web Server). We are collecting your IP Address, type of platform device, operating system, screen resolution and web browser. These data/information are not manually created and logged instead it's automatically created and logged by the analytic tool and the web server we use. We may need these data to monitor and prevent any kind of suspicious activities.

    We collect your demographics

    We collect some of your demographics by using third-party app such as Google Analytics (User Analytic Tool). We are collecting which city & country you live at current time, it is based on your current IP Address/ISP. Your gender & age are never being collected. These data/information are not manually created and logged instead it's automatically created and logged by the analytic tool we use. We may need these data to monitor and prevent any kind of suspicious activities.

    We collect your engagement activities

    We collect some of your engagement activites by using third-party app such as Google Analytics (User Analytic Tool) & Google Tag Manager (Tagging Tool). We are collecting your first time to visit the website (first_visit), how many times your browser load/visit of each page (page_view), how many times you click any button/link of each page (click), your first time scrolling from the top to bottom of each page (scroll). If you see the text in brackets and has been bolded, we called it events, and there are 2 (user_engagement & session_start) Google Analytics events to collect your engagement activities but we can't explain about them, you can learn more about these events through this link. These data/information are not manually created and logged instead it's automatically created and logged by the analytic & tagging tool we use. We may need these data to review and improve our UI website in accessibility, functionality and comforts for our visitors.
